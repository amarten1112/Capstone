Design Test: Chaotic/Trendy 2026 Style

This header prototype explores 2026 design trends with chaotic elements including angled strips, floating circles, wavy lines, and playful typography while maintaining a farmers market brand identity. The design breaks strict traditional rules but still uses key design elements: line, shape, color, space, emphasis, and movement in a more dynamic, energetic way.

Design Elements Used: Bold color contrast (green, gold, blue), rotated/skewed text, layered transparent shapes, playful hover effects, and dynamic positioning.
